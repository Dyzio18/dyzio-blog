Hello! I'm Patrick, a frontend developer with a passion for travel. Currently, I live in Warsaw, but my journey began in Biłgoraj, a small (but great) town in East Poland, where I was born in 1995. I also had the pleasure of living in the beautiful city of Cracow due my studies at the AGH University of Science and Technology. I spend lovely time in Cracow, but I decided to move to Warsaw in 2022 to start a new chapter in my life.


On this blog, you can find my travel reportages and posts about programming and AI.

I am the founder of [eqchange.pl](https://www.eqchange.pl), a hyperlocal app for free exchanges and local communities.

Since this is my personal blog, most of the articles are in Polish, but you might find some posts in English from time to time. I invite you to read, engage, and share your thoughts. Let's connect and learn together!
